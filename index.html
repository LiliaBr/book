About this book

The underlying goal of "Machine Learning Simplified" is to develop strong intuition fr ML inside you. We would use simple intuitive examples to explain complex concepts, algorithms or methods, as well as democratize all mathematics behind machine learning.

After reading this book, you would understand everything that comes into the scope of Supervised ML, and would be able to not only understand nitty-gritty details of mathematics behind the scene, but also explain to anyone how things work on a high level.
